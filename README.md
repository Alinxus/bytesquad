#  Nera

**Getting paid internationally as an African freelancer shouldn’t be this hard.**

Nera is a payments back-office built for African freelancers and small teams — simplifying how they **receive, manage, split, and track payments across borders.**

---

## The Problem

A freelancer in Lagos works for a client in London.

Sounds simple… until payment happens.

-  High fees from platforms like Wise or PayPal  
-  Poor exchange rates  
-  Difficulty moving money into local bank accounts  
-  Manual stress when splitting payments with teammates  
-  No clean records for taxes or compliance  

**Result:** Getting paid internationally becomes messy, expensive, and inefficient.

---

##  The Solution — Nera

Nera acts as the **financial layer between freelancers and the chaos of global payments.**

Think of it as your **payments back-office**.

---

## What You Can Do With Nera

### 1. Create Invoices & Payment Links
- Send professional invoices to international clients  
- Accept payments in **USD, GBP, EUR**, etc.  
- Smooth, clean experience for clients  

---

### 2.  Accept Local Payments
- Receive payments within Africa  
- Support for:
  - Bank transfers  
  - Mobile money  
  - Local payment methods  

---

### 3. Smart Currency Conversion
- Convert earnings at **fair exchange rates**  
- Route funds seamlessly to:
  - Local bank accounts  
  - Mobile wallets  
  - Nera balance  

---

### 4. Automatic Payment Splitting
- Working with a team? No problem  
- Define payout splits upfront  
- Everyone gets paid automatically  

---

### 5. Unified Payment Tracking
- Track all:
  - Payments  
  - Conversions  
  - Settlements  
- No more digging through emails or bank statements  

---

### 6. Compliance & Tax Records
- Export clean financial reports  
- Ready for:
  - Tax filing  
  - Proof of income  
  - Audits  

---

## Who It's For

- Freelance Developers

**Basically:** Anyone earning in multiple currencies and dealing with cross-border payments.

## Business Model

Nera makes money through:
- Small fees on **currency conversions**
- Small fees on **payouts**

The more users earn through Nera, the more Nera scales.
